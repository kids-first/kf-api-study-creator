Model
=====

Study
-----

A study mimics a study within the Data Service. It encompases a group of
similarly collected data.

Batch
-----

A batch is a group of data within a study. A batch may suppliment a study's
data through augmentation, alteration, or removal.

File Essence
------------

A file essense symbolizes the ideal and complete formulation of a specific
subset of a batch's data. The essence of a file may need to be iterated on,
improving the underlying representation to better match the true form of the
file.

Object
------

An object is a materialization of a file's essence in the form of an on-disk
representation.
